Campfire Workshop

Crafting details and workshop unlocks for the Summer Campfire Event.

How It Works

Once you have your campfire roaring, head over to the Campfire Workshop to get your DIY crafting going.

Getting your campfire burning hotter unlocks higher crafting tiers. Each tier unlocks new crafting recipes. These recipes are only craftable, and the timers only continue, while the appropriate campfire tier is burning.

Keep that campfire fueled and burning so your crafts keep earning. Recipes can be found under the plant, pet, gear, and cosmetic pages.
