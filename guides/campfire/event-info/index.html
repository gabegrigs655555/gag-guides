Event Info

Overview for the Summer Campfire Event and its weekly visitors.

Summer Campfire Event

Get your camping gear ready as the Summer Campfire Event is here. Warm temperatures and cozy nights mean it is time to gather around the Summer Campfire and roast your garden harvest. With new seeds, pets, and cosmetics to unlock, there is plenty to earn during the camping trip.

Rewards are earned through crafting, so bring some DIY energy and visit Camper Carl, the Summer Campfire expert, to begin.

Bearnaby Arrives

A hungry grizzly visitor has arrived for Summer Campfire event part two. Bearnaby is here to take scouts campfire foraging. Talk to him to begin and receive exclusive campfire seeds, with a chance at sought-after rainbow variants.

Campfire Keeper

Part three brings a mysterious guest and new DIY recipes to the Campfire Workshop. The Campfire Keeper has noticed your contributions to the heat of the campfire and proposes a ritual to make your pet more powerful.

Gather the required items from existing campfire friends and bring a pet to discover the new pet mutation.

Park Ranger

Your campfire may have been too hot. For the final week, Park Ranger joins the summer camping trip with new tasks and rewards.

A new collection log has also been added. Fill up the collection and walk away with an exclusive campfire souvenir.
