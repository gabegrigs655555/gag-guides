Crop Submissions

Bearnaby would like your help. Bring him foraged crops from your garden to satisfy his appetite. He will ask for crops with a specific mutation, trait, and weight.

Handing in the crop he wants earns an exclusive Campfire Seed Pack, with an Exotic Campfire Seed Pack every 50 submissions. Bearnaby also awards an Inferno Shard for every 25 plant submissions.

Plant mutations and weights change as you progress through submissions. Requested crop traits alternate every 30 minutes.
