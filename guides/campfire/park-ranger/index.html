Park Ranger Quests

Park Ranger is here to inspect your camp grounds. Campfire quests are here for the event: complete quests related to the campfire, finish them all, and claim a reward.

Quests are given at random from easy, medium, and hard difficulty and refresh about every 10 minutes.

Completing 3 quests grants a choice between three reward options. Park Ranger may even have an exclusive new plant waiting.
